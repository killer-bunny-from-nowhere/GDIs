**Crocodylus porosus**


The total length of 495 cm represents the asymptotic length given by Webb & Manolis (1989)[^1] and accepted by Fukuda et al. (2013)[^2]

[^1]: Webb, G., & Manolis, S. C. (1989). Crocodiles of Australia. (No Title).
[^2]: Fukuda, Y., Saalfeld, K., Lindner, G., & Nichols, T. (2013). Estimation of total length from head length of saltwater crocodiles (Crocodylus porosus) in the Northern Territory, Australia. Journal of Herpetology, 47(1), 34-40.
